Best Hotels in Pune

Pune offers a wide range of accommodations to suit every budget and preference. Here are some of the best hotels in Pune:

1. JW Marriott Hotel Pune: This luxury hotel offers upscale rooms, multiple dining options, and a rooftop bar with stunning city views. Amenities include a spa, a fitness center, and an outdoor pool.

2. The Westin Pune Koregaon Park: Known for its modern architecture and luxurious rooms, The Westin offers several dining options, a fitness studio, and a rooftop pool. It’s situated in the vibrant Koregaon Park area, known for its nightlife and dining.

3. Conrad Pune: A luxury hotel with spacious rooms, Conrad Pune features multiple dining options, a 24-hour fitness center, and an outdoor pool. Its central location makes it ideal for business and leisure travelers.

4. Hyatt Pune: Located near the Pune Airport, this hotel offers comfortable rooms, a spa, a fitness center, and an outdoor pool. Its convenient location makes it a popular choice for travelers.

5. The Ritz-Carlton, Pune: This opulent hotel provides a blend of luxury and comfort with its elegant rooms, fine dining options, a rooftop bar, and a world-class spa. It’s located near the Pune Golf Course, adding to its appeal for business and leisure guests.

6. Marriott Suites Pune: Ideal for extended stays, this hotel offers spacious suites with kitchenettes, a fitness center, a pool, and multiple dining options. Its location in Koregaon Park makes it a convenient choice for long-term visitors.

7. Sheraton Grand Pune Bund Garden Hotel: Combining classic elegance with modern amenities, this hotel features comfortable rooms, several dining options, a fitness center, and an outdoor pool. It’s centrally located, offering easy access to Pune’s attractions.

8. Radisson Blu Hotel Pune Kharadi: This hotel offers stylish rooms, a variety of dining options, a fitness center, and an outdoor pool. Its location near IT hubs makes it a preferred choice for business travelers.

9. O Hotel Pune: Located in Koregaon Park, O Hotel offers chic rooms, a rooftop bar, a spa, and a fitness center. Its contemporary design and convenient location make it popular among travelers.

10. Four Points by Sheraton Hotel & Serviced Apartments Pune: Offering a range of rooms and serviced apartments, this hotel provides comfort and convenience with its dining options, fitness center, and outdoor pool. It’s located near the airport and major business districts.
