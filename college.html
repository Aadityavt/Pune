Top Engineering Colleges in Pune

D.I.A.T. (Defence Institute of Advanced Technology)

Researchers at D.I.A.T. have published more than 2000 papers and submitted over 50 patent applications to the Indian Patent Office. Patrick Blackett and Daulat Singh Kothari founded the Indian Armament Studies, later known as the Institute of Armament Technology, in 1952.

History: Patrick Blackett, Nobel Prize winner in Physics (1948), and Daulat Singh Kothari, Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan recipient, founded Indian Armament Studies.

College of Engineering, Pune (COEP)

A Unitary Public University of the Government of Maharashtra, COEP is a technological University located in Pune. It was founded in July 1854 and is known for training public works department employees.

History: Founded as Poona Engineering and Mechanical School, later renamed Poona Civil Engineering College in 1865.

M.I.T. World Peace University

A state-owned private university, MIT-WPU is located in Kothrud, Pune. It was established following Maharashtra Government Act No. XXXV 2017.

History: Previously known as Maharashtra Institute of Technology, it is part of the M.I.T. Group of Institutions.
